Quick Solar Facts for North Texas

229 Sunny Days Per Year

North Texas receives among the most sunlight of any region in the country, making Wise, Parker, Jack, and Montague counties excellent solar markets.

30% Federal Tax Credit

The federal Investment Tax Credit reduces your solar installation cost by 30%, dollar for dollar off your federal tax bill. Available through 2032 for residential systems.

7–9 Year Payback Period

Most North Texas homeowners recoup their solar investment in 7 to 9 years. With panels warranted for 25–30 years, that leaves 15–20+ years of near-free electricity.

Oncor Net Metering

Most of North Texas falls under Oncor's distribution territory. Understanding which Retail Electric Provider to choose for your solar buyback plan is critical to maximizing your savings.

Starting at $2.40/Watt

N-Tech Energy Solutions prices start well below the Texas state average. A 10kW system starts at $24,000 before the 30% tax credit, reducing your net cost to around $16,800.

Battery Backup for Texas Storms

Grid-tied solar systems shut off during outages. Adding a battery (Tesla Powerwall, Enphase IQ) keeps your home powered when North Texas storms knock out the grid.
