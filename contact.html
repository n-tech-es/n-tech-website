What Happens After You Contact Us

1. We Respond Within 24 Hours

Every message is reviewed by a real person on our team, not a bot. We'll reach out by your preferred contact method — phone, email, or text — within one business day. Most inquiries get a same-day response during business hours.

2. Free Energy Consultation

If you're interested in a solar quote, we'll schedule a free consultation. We review your electric bills, analyze your roof using satellite imagery, and provide a custom system design with transparent pricing — no home visit required.

3. No Pressure, Ever

We don't use commissioned salespeople or high-pressure tactics. If you reach out with a question, we answer it honestly. If you want a quote, we give you one. If you decide solar isn't right for you right now, we respect that completely.

Why Homeowners in North Texas Choose N-Tech

N-Tech Energy Solutions was built from the ground up to serve Wise County, Parker County, Jack County, and Montague County. We're not a national company with a call center — we're a local solar provider that knows the communities, utilities, and permitting requirements in our service area. Our pricing starts at $2.40 per watt, below the Texas average, because our lean model eliminates unnecessary overhead.

Every customer receives a personalized system design based on their actual roof dimensions, orientation, shading, and energy usage history. We handle all permits, Oncor interconnection paperwork, and utility coordination on your behalf. From your first inquiry to the day your panels turn on, you'll have one point of contact who knows your project.

Frequently Asked Contact Questions

Do you charge for consultations?

No. Initial consultations and solar design proposals are completely free with no obligation. You only pay if you decide to move forward with an installation, and only after reviewing and approving your custom quote.

What's the fastest way to get a quote?

Fill out our free energy consultation form at the link above — it takes less than two minutes. You can also call (214) 267-9372 directly during business hours (Mon–Fri 8am–7pm, Sat 9am–4pm) and speak with someone immediately.

Do you serve my area?

We serve Wise County, Parker County, Jack County, Montague County, and surrounding communities within about 60 miles of Chico, TX. If you're unsure whether we cover your area, just ask — we're always happy to check and are actively expanding our coverage.

Can I email instead of calling?

Absolutely. Reach us any time at customerexperience@n-tech-es.com. We respond to all emails within one business day, and for detailed questions, email is often more convenient than a phone call.
