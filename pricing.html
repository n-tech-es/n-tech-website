Pricing Questions Answered

Are there any hidden fees?

No. Our per-watt pricing includes everything: equipment, installation, permits, engineering, utility paperwork, and monitoring. The only potential additional cost would be if your site requires unexpected structural work, which we'd identify upfront.

How does the 30% federal tax credit work?

The federal solar tax credit (ITC) gives you 30% back on your total system cost. For example, a $25,000 system qualifies for a $7,500 tax credit, reducing your net cost to $17,500. This is claimed when you file your federal taxes.

Do you offer financing?

Yes! We partner with leading solar financing companies to offer options including 0% APR for qualified buyers, 12-25 year terms, and no prepayment penalties. We'll help you find the best option for your situation.

What size system do I need?

Most North Texas homes need between 8-12kW. We'll analyze your actual electric bills and roof space to recommend the right size. Get a free personalized quote to see your exact recommendation.

What's the difference between the plans?

Higher tiers include more efficient panels (generating more power in less space), longer warranties, and better monitoring systems. All plans use quality equipment and professional installation - you're choosing the level of efficiency and coverage that fits your needs.

Can I get a custom quote?

Absolutely! Every home is different. Our pricing tiers are starting points - we'll create a custom design and quote specific to your roof, energy usage, and goals.
