What We Stand For

Five principles we refuse to compromise on.

Transparency First

Our plan-based pricing means no surprise quotes, no hidden fees, and no wondering if you got a fair deal. What you see is exactly what you pay.

Local Partnerships

We work exclusively with licensed Texas electrical contractors who know North Texas homes, weather, and utility requirements inside and out.

No Pressure Sales

No commissioned sales reps. No door knockers. No follow-up harassment. You decide on your timeline and we respect that completely.

Ownership, Not Leases

We only do cash and financing — no leases, no PPAs. Your system is yours from day one. Texas solar tax exemptions apply to you as the owner. And when your home sells, owned solar adds value instead of complicating the deal.

Texas-Specific Expertise

We understand ERCOT, Texas utility incentives, local permitting, and North Texas roof types. This isn't a generic solar company — we're built for here.

Our Story

2025

The idea takes shape

Nathan Nwosu, an electrical engineer and Texas resident, noticed a gap in the North Texas solar market — homeowners were either getting oversold by aggressive companies or overwhelmed by confusing options. The vision for N-Tech was born: a remote-first solar company that puts the customer first.

Early 2026

N-Tech Energy Solutions LLC is formed

The company is officially registered as a Texas LLC with a clear mission — bring transparent, plan-based solar to Wise County, Parker County, and surrounding North Texas communities.

February 2026

First installer partnership signed

N-Tech partners with Will Power Electric LLC — a licensed Texas electrical contractor based in the Chico TX area — to begin delivering projects across a 40-mile service radius.

Now

Actively serving North Texas

N-Tech is open for business and actively working with homeowners across Wise and Parker County. The founding customer program is live — and the first 25 customers get exclusive pricing and perks.

Common Questions About N-Tech

What counties do you serve?

We primarily serve Wise County, Parker County, Jack County, and Montague County in North Texas. Our installer partner operates out of Chico, TX within a roughly 60-mile service radius. If you're unsure whether we cover your area, reach out at (214) 267-9372 or customerexperience@n-tech-es.com.

How is N-Tech different from other solar companies?

Most solar companies rely on commissioned salespeople, high overhead, and opaque pricing. N-Tech operates entirely online with a flat per-watt pricing model that starts at $2.40/watt. You always know exactly what you're paying, and we never use pressure tactics. We're local to North Texas and built specifically for homeowners here.

Who actually installs the system?

Physical installation is performed by licensed electrical contractors we've vetted in each service area. N-Tech manages your project from first call through permission to operate: design, permits, utility interconnection, and scheduling. You have one point of contact the entire time — us. The difference between our model and a national company isn't that we work with contractors — it's that we know exactly who's going to your roof, we've vetted them, and they're local to your area. That's not the same as a national company farming your job to whoever bids lowest in your zip code.
