The 5-Step Process

Get Your Free Consultation

Enter your address and average electric bill on our website. We'll use satellite imagery to analyze your roof and provide an instant savings estimate with system recommendations - all online, no home visit required.

Takes 5 minutes

Review Your Custom Design

We create a detailed solar design specific to your roof, including panel placement, system size, production estimates, and exact pricing. Review everything online. Have questions? Schedule a video call or phone consultation at your convenience.

1-2 business days

We Handle All Paperwork

Once you're ready to move forward, we take care of everything: engineering stamps, permit applications, utility interconnection forms, and HOA approvals (if applicable). You just sign a few digital documents - we do the rest.

1-2 weeks
3
4

Professional Installation

Our licensed installer partner arrives with all equipment and completes your installation in 3-5 business days. Clean, professional work. Daily site cleanup. Minimal disruption to your routine. We'll coordinate the inspection with your local authority.

3-5 days

Start Saving Money

After passing inspection, your utility grants Permission to Operate (PTO). Flip the switch and watch your electric meter run backwards! You'll have access to your monitoring system to track production and savings in real-time.
