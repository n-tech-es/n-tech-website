Why Azle Electric Bills Run High

Azle is a community that blends suburban convenience with rural character — and its housing stock ranges from older ranch-style homes to newer developments along major corridors. Across all these home types, the energy efficiency patterns are similar: under-insulated attics, ductwork that's never been pressure-tested, and HVAC systems working harder than they need to because the home isn't well-sealed.

CoServ serves a significant portion of the Azle area, and with utility rates increasing, the cost of those inefficiencies shows up more clearly in your monthly bill than it did even a few years ago. The good news: most of the fixes are straightforward and affordable once you know exactly what they are.

N-Tech identifies every energy loss in your home, ranks them by cost-to-fix vs. dollars saved, and delivers your written report within 48 hours. You'll never guess at your bill again.

What We Check

Most energy audits hand you a 40-page PDF you'll never read. Ours gives you a one-page prioritized action list. We cover:

  • Attic insulation & ventilation
  • Air sealing & infiltration (drafts)
  • HVAC system age & efficiency
  • Ductwork condition
  • Water heater type & age
  • Windows & doors
  • Lighting & appliances
  • Electrical panel (including solar readiness)
  • Photos of all major findings included

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I have to do anything before the audit?

No. Just make sure someone 18+ is home and the auditor has access to the attic and main electrical panel.

How is this different from what my utility offers?

Utility energy tools are often virtual or checklist-based. Our audit is an in-person visit with a certified professional, photos, a written report, and a personal follow-up call.

Do you pressure us to buy solar?

No. The audit stands on its own. The report is yours regardless of what you decide.

Are you licensed to do energy audits?

Our auditors carry BPI Building Analyst certification or equivalent credentials. Texas does not require a state license for residential energy auditing.
