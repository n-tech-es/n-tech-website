## Federal Tax Credit (ITC)

The 30% federal Investment Tax Credit reduces your federal income tax liability dollar-for-dollar.

- 30% credit through 2032 (per current law)
- On a $25,000 system: **$7,500 credit**
- On a $29,000 system: **$8,700 credit**
- Applies to equipment + installation costs combined
- Tax credit availability varies by individual tax situation — consult a tax professional
