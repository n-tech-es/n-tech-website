Why North Texas Is an Ideal Solar Market

North Texas consistently ranks among the best regions in the United States for solar energy production. With an average of 229 sunny days per year, homeowners in Decatur, Bridgeport, Weatherford, Jacksboro, Bowie, and surrounding communities can generate substantial electricity from a rooftop solar system. The Texas sun is a natural resource that can be captured and converted into real monthly savings on your Oncor electricity bill.

Electricity rates in North Texas have risen steadily year after year. By generating your own power and locking in a fixed cost through solar, you effectively hedge against future utility rate increases. Texas offers a sales tax exemption on solar equipment and a homestead property tax exemption on any home value solar adds — and net metering buyback is available from several Texas Retail Electric Providers. The financial case for solar in this region can be compelling for the right home and energy profile.

N-Tech Energy Solutions prices starting at $2.40 per watt — significantly below the Texas state average — because our lean, community-focused model eliminates the overhead costs that larger national companies pass on to customers. We serve each county in our service area with the same attention and care, whether you're in a city like Weatherford or a rural area of Montague County.

Service Area Questions

Do you serve rural properties and ranches?

Yes. We regularly work with homeowners on larger rural properties throughout Wise County, Jack County, and Montague County. Rural properties often have excellent roof orientation and minimal shading, making them ideal candidates for solar. Large properties may also be good candidates for ground-mounted systems if roof space is limited or not ideally oriented.

Can I get solar in a subdivision with an HOA?

Texas state law (HB 362) generally protects homeowners' rights to install solar panels in HOA-governed communities, with some restrictions on placement for aesthetic reasons. We handle all HOA approval applications on your behalf as part of our service — there's no additional charge for this.
