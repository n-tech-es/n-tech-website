What Happens After You Submit

Step 1: Review (Within 24 Hours)

A real person on our team reviews your submission and pulls satellite data for your home address. We analyze your roof size, orientation, and estimated sun exposure to begin building your custom design.

Step 2: Custom Design

We build a solar design specific to your home — panel placement, system size, annual production estimates, and a complete price breakdown starting at $2.40 per watt. You receive this via email with no obligation.

Step 3: Consultation Call

We schedule a brief call to walk through your design, answer questions, and discuss financing options. This call is relaxed and educational — we're here to inform, not to pressure you into a decision.

Step 4: Your Decision

Take as much time as you need. If you decide to move forward, we begin the permitting and installation process. If you're not ready, there's no pressure — we'll be here when you are.

Why Choose N-Tech Energy Solutions

N-Tech Energy Solutions was built specifically for homeowners in Wise County, Parker County, Jack County, and Montague County, Texas. We understand Oncor's interconnection requirements, the local permitting process, and the specific solar conditions in North Texas. Our pricing starts at $2.40 per watt — below the Texas state average — because our lean operating model passes savings directly to you.

We don't use commissioned door-knockers, high-pressure sales tactics, or surprise fees. Every cost in your quote is explained before you sign anything. We handle all permits, utility paperwork, and Oncor interconnection on your behalf. Common Questions About Getting a Quote

Is the quote really free?

Yes, completely free. No credit card, no commitment, no follow-up pressure. We provide your custom design and savings estimate at no cost because we want you to have the information you need to make a good decision.

How accurate is the estimate?

Our initial estimate is based on satellite imagery, your electric bill amount, and sun data for your specific location. It's highly accurate for most homes. Once we confirm your exact roof dimensions and Oncor account details, we finalize the numbers — typically within 5% of the initial estimate.

How long does the quote process take?

Most homeowners receive their custom design within one business day of submitting this form. We'll schedule a 20-minute call at your convenience to review everything together. The entire process from form submission to receiving your quote takes less than 48 hours in most cases.

Do I have to be home for you to come out?

No home visit is required for the quote process. We work entirely remotely using satellite imagery and your submitted information. If you decide to move forward, we'll coordinate installation scheduling with you — no surprise visits.
