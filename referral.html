Common Questions

When do I get paid?

Rewards are paid after your referral's installation is complete and their payment has been received. For energy audit referrals, payment follows completed audit delivery. We pay via Zelle, check, or your preferred method — typically within 5 business days of job completion.

Is there a limit on how many people I can refer?

No limit. Refer as many neighbors, friends, or clients as you'd like. Each closed installation or audit qualifies for a separate reward payment.

Does the person I refer have to commit right away?

No. They can take as much time as they need. As long as you submitted their name through our referral form before they signed a contract with us, you'll receive credit for the referral. We don't put pressure on your contacts — that's the whole point.

Who qualifies as a trade partner?

Any professional who regularly works with North Texas homeowners — realtors, HVAC technicians, roofers, electricians, home inspectors, property managers, and similar trades. If you're interacting with homeowners and want to add value to your clients while earning a referral fee, you qualify.

What if my referral gets an energy audit instead of solar?

Energy audit referrals qualify for a $50 reward (homeowners) or $75 reward (trade partners) per completed audit. The audit is often where people start before deciding on solar — and that's fine. We're happy to serve them wherever they are in the process.

Do I have to be an existing N-Tech customer to refer someone?

No. Anyone in our service area — homeowner, neighbor, or trade professional — can participate. You don't need to have gone solar yourself to make an introduction.
