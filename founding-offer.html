Common Questions

Is there any cost to apply?

Zero. Applying for a consultation is completely free with no obligation. You only pay when you decide to move forward with an installation.

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve homeowners within a 40-mile radius of Chico, TX — covering Wise County, Parker County, and surrounding North Texas communities including Decatur, Bridgeport, Azle, Weatherford, and more.

How long does the process take?

From consultation to an energized system typically takes 2-4 weeks. Founding customers receive priority scheduling which can speed that timeline up significantly.

What if I'm just researching?

That's perfectly fine. Fill out the form and we'll give you a free consultation with zero pressure. You'll leave knowing exactly what solar would cost and save for your specific home — useful information regardless of your timeline.

Do I need to own my home?

Yes — solar installations require homeowner consent and typically homeownership. If you're renting, we'd need written permission from your landlord to proceed.

What is the founding discount and how much can I save?

The founding customer discount is applied to your total system cost before the federal tax credit. Combined with the 30% ITC, founding customers in Wise County, Parker County, Jack County, and Montague County can see significantly lower out-of-pocket costs than standard customers who come to us later. We share the exact discount amount during your consultation because every system size is different. At our base rate of $2.40/watt, a 10kW system starts at $24,000 before any discounts or tax credits — the founding offer brings that number down further.

Why does it matter to be a founding customer?

When a company is new, the first customers take on a small amount of trust. In exchange, we reward that trust with our best pricing, our most personal service, and priority scheduling. Our founding customers also help shape how we grow — their feedback guides our process improvements. This is a genuinely limited offer: once 25 installations are complete, the founding pricing closes permanently and standard rates apply to all new customers going forward. There is no way to unlock founding pricing after the offer closes.
