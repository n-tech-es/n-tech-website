Common Questions

Is there any cost to apply?

Zero. Applying for a consultation is completely free with no obligation. You only pay when you decide to move forward with an installation.

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve homeowners within a 40-mile radius of Chico, TX — covering Wise County, Parker County, and surrounding North Texas communities including Decatur, Bridgeport, Azle, Weatherford, and more.

How long does the process take?

From consultation to an energized system typically takes 2-4 weeks. Founding customers receive priority scheduling which can speed that timeline up significantly.

What if I'm just researching?

That's perfectly fine. Fill out the form and we'll give you a free consultation with zero pressure. You'll leave knowing exactly what solar would cost and save for your specific home — useful information regardless of your timeline.

Do I need to own my home?

Yes — solar installations require homeowner consent and typically homeownership. If you're renting, we'd need written permission from your landlord to proceed.
